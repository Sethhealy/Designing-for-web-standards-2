About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to create unforgettable events by exceeding your expectations with innovative cuisine, exceptional service and creative presentation. We will accomplish this successfully by consistently. We are a family owned and operated company based out of Orlando,Fl the catering side of the company came from our years of working at restaurants and wanting a place of our own. We are a small married couple but we are very interested in your business.

Going Green

Gardin Variety is committed to Environmentally Friendly Practices both in our office and at our catering events. We are proud to have been a pioneer of the “Green Movement” in Phoenix. By using biodegradable plates, napkins, utensils, cups, and to-go containers at catered events, virtually eliminating the use of water bottles, recycling in the office and at catering events, and partnering with local merchants and farmers we have been able to greatly reduce our carbon footprint. We pride ourselves on using local products produced naturally and organically by small family farmers, ranchers, and herdsmen whenever possible, supporting an environmentally sound food system where foods produced locally are consumed locally. While Gardin Variety is proud of the progress we have made, we are constantly working to further develop and better our environmental practices as new products become available.
